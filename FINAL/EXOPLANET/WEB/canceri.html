55 CANCERI E

This super-Earth offers insights into planetary formation and composition

Key Characteristics

Habitability: No, extreme temperatures.

Land: Rocky surface, possible diamond composition.

Gases: Carbon dioxide, nitrogen, helium.

Constellation: Cancer

Sky Appearance: Red-orange due to star's color.

Structure: Solid surface, iron core, silicate crust.

Magnetic Field Aurora: Weak, possible induction from star.

More Quick Facts

Mass: 8.6 Earth masses

Radius: 2.17 Earth radii

Temperature:Surface temperature 3,900°F (2,148°C)

Star type: G8V (yellow dwarf)

Also Known For

1. Tidal locking (one side constantly faces star)
2. Possible diamond composition
3. Extreme surface temperature
