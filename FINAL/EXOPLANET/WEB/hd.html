Key Characteristics

Habitability: No, extreme temperatures.

Land: No solid surface, gaseous.

Gases: Hydrogen, helium, sodium, potassium, water vapor.

Constellation: Vulpecula

Sky Appearance: Deep blue due to atmospheric scattering.

Structure: Gas giant, no solid core.

Magnetic Field Aurora: Strong, complex magnetic field.

Mass: 1.13 Jupiter masses

Radius: 1.26 Jupiter radii

Temperature:Surface temperature 1832°F (1000°C)

Atmospheric escape: Detected

Star type: K-type main-sequence (orange dwarf)
